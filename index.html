How Breathing Reduces Stress

Activates Parasympathetic System

Deep breathing triggers your body's relaxation response, slowing heart rate and lowering blood pressure. It signals your brain that it's safe to calm down.

Increases Oxygen Flow

Controlled breathing delivers more oxygen to your brain and muscles, improving clarity and reducing physical tension. Your body functions better when properly oxygenated.

Promotes Mindfulness

Focusing on your breath anchors you in the present moment, breaking the cycle of anxious thoughts. It's a form of meditation that's always available to you.
